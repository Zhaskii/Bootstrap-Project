Introduction

KAUTILYA GLOBAL SCHOOL: was established in 2052 B.S. with the view to maintain true modern day marvel, one of the best and high standard quality education centers catering at the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal. It has been upgraded as Global Standard School with its aim to provide quality education with an activity - base learning programmes.

KAUTILYA GLOBAL SCHOOL

KAUTILYA GLOBAL SCHOOL: possesses ample scope for expansion and aspiration to develop more academic programmes assisted by modern technology in school and higher levels.

The School has excellent infrastructure supported by state of the art facilities such as modern academic buildings with spacious classrooms, laboratories, conference hall, administrative offices, spacious playing ground and so on. As a matter of fact , the school will surely become the pride of the nation in future. We have committed to put our heart and soul into the execution of the noble solemn and sacred task.

Massage From Principle

I am privileged to be a part of KAUTILYA GLOBAL SCHOOL, which I reckon has to come a long way. As we have a common vision to establish a unique institution providing quality education of an International standard and focusing on a holistic education, I am quite optimistic that it will stand out from the existing institutions in the country . I would like to assure you that I will leave no stone unturned to take this institution to a great height of success.

I fully assure you that I will work with full commitment and dedication and will encourage each member of the staff to be filled with strong zeal and vigor for the all-round development of students, who will be left in our care.

Furthermore, I take this opportunity to disseminate some important information on KAUTILYA GLOBAL SCHOOL. The journey of learning is a never-ending process. However, it is important how we develop the habit and attitude in the young minds. Our team’s best effort will be focused with the belief that every child is a unique individual with their own characteristics and potentiality, which can be groomed with practical methods, varieties of activities and moral education so as to develop their psychological and physical needs.

The aim of KAUTILYA GLOBAL SCHOOL is to provide the best school environment, where the pupils feel secure and develop an attitude of self reliance and self-discipline. We support their initiation to explore their potential and express their feelings and their desires.

In this modern world, it is pertinent to their creativity for the holistic development of a person with positive thoughts, feelings and imagination. There is a need for children to be cared for and nurtured so as to make them responsible citizens and act as reliable members in a community coupled with morality and fairness.

I welcome your child to this Academy and congratulate you on your decision to make it your choice. So, let’s give your children an opportunity to have a life-long practical education, which emphasizes human values beneficial not only to the learners but also to the society in which they live.

B.D. Nirola

Principal
